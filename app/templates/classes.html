CLASSES

Shows a grid of classes as well as the soonest thing due for that class. The last class in the grid will be a placeholder that lets you add or remove another class

If you click a class, it will open that class and show the assignments for it. If you are a teacher who has a class, inside you should be able to see the number of students who have looked at, started, or completed an assignment. Also you should be able to "ping" that assignment to let your students know that you think it should be done soon (they can silence pings without the teacher knowing that they silenced them)
